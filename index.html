Advanced Medical Imaging Lab

School of Life Science and Technology, Xidian University

We develop and apply novel imaging techniques and image analysis methods to enable improved understanding of human diseases and human brain. With the integrated expertise in engineering, physics, chemistry, biology, medicine, and entrepreneurship, our research areas are devoted to the development of multiscale biophtonics imaging techniques, development of devices for early diagnosis and precise treatment, cognitive neuroscience and hybrid artificial intelligence.
